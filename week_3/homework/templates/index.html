Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Rules:

To play, you will need 2 players! Each player makes a choice between rock, paper or scissors.

  • If you choose rock, you will win if your opponent chooses scissors. You will lose if your opponent chooses paper.
  • If you choose paper, you will win if your opponent chooses rock. You will lose if your opponent chooses scissors.
  • If you choose scissors, you will win if your opponent chooses paper. You will lose if your opponent chooses rock.

Hit the Play button at the top of the page to play your game!
